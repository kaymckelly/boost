Welcome to Boost! This is our mascot, a happy piece of toast. (Yeah, we know: it's cute.)


Boost helps you do three things:

  • Boost your own self-esteem
  • Encourage you to boost others' self-esteem
  • Teach a variety of self-care techniques


You're not required to log in; the self-care features are publicly available. But the core functionality - boosting your and others' self-esteem - requires you to be logged in. We use your Instagram account to verify your identity, so trolls can be held responsible should there be any.
